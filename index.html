About me

Hi I'm Sara!
I'm a graphic design student at MTSU, graduating in 2024. I was born in Egypt & raised in Nashville. I want to focus my career on illustration and game development. I have experience in Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, Blender 3d, Unity, HTML, CSS, and JS.

I am Adobe Illustrator certified on Linkedin Learning and I have earned the Unity Essentials and Junior Programmer badges on Unity Learn.
1 engineering major + 1 horrible semester = 1 art major with a strange transcript

Time

September 2021
A project that explores time through units and increments. What better way to show time passing than the ever-changing sky? I used a modular system of triangles to show the sky on different dates, sunlight through a window, and the daylight cycle. I used and broke the grid in the form of a gif, poster, and booklet. Made in Illustrator and Blender.
